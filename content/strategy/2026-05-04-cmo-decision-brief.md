# Email Program — Decision Brief

## Recommendation

**Split email in two: Transactional (LLM-generated, auto-ship after rubric) and Editorial (human-authored with LLM data + voice-pass assist). Park whale-tier until data pipeline, editor capacity, and Phase 2 CIO auth land.**

## The 3 strongest supporting findings

- **Your only clean ship was the most transactional email.** Pack Received v1001 — `ship`, no notes. It also has the most behavioral data and zero personal-letter framing. Every email you rejected hardest (Whale "absolutely terrible," Reactivation "need real specifics," Drop Announcement template-fatigue) was voice-load-bearing. *(Wave 1A §2)*
- **The industry data converges.** Skybox Collectibles — closest analog — hit 63.6% open rate without LLM copy generation; behavioral triggers + collector voice did the work. Across 200+ campaigns raw LLM underperforms human by 18%. Gmail's Feb 2026 AI-spam filter penalizes LLM-similarity at 2.4×. *(Wave 1B §2.5, §4.1, §4.7)*
- **Your 80/20 voice ratio IS the split.** Verbatim: *"80–90% platform voice, 10–20% Magic personal."* Platform voice = LLM grounded in data = transactional. Magic-personal at template-scale fails: "1,122 collectors receiving the same template is a broadcast pretending to be personal." *(Wave 1A §1)*

## The 3 strongest objections, pre-empted

- **"Iterate the prompt once more?"** Four iterations rejected. This is an architecture failure, not a prompt failure. A fifth iteration of the same architecture = a fifth rejection. *(Wave 1B §8.1)*
- **"Doesn't this slow editorial output?"** Yes, by design, where speed costs trust. The 1,122 dormant collectors hold $234.6M GMV — a Sam-authored Run It Back that lifts spend beats LLM volume they ignore. Transactional ships at speed.
- **"Whale deferral leaves money on the table."** Deferral protects 66-83% of monthly revenue. Premature LLM whale-send is unrecoverable. Three concrete unblocks gate re-entry, not vibes. *(Wave 1A §3.A)*

## Decision

**Approve the two-tier split (whale-tier deferred), yes / no / modify?**
